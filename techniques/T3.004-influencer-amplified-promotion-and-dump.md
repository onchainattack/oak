# OAK-T3.004 — Influencer-Amplified Promotion-and-Dump

**Parent Tactics:** OAK-T3 (Pump-and-Dump and Market Manipulation), OAK-T17 (Market Manipulation)
**Maturity:** emerging
**Chains:** EVM (Ethereum, Solana via Pump.fun, BNB Chain); cross-chain
**First documented:** 2021-06 (Save the Kids / $KIDS — FaZe Clan / Frazier Kay / RiceGum / Sommer Ray); 2021-12 (CryptoZoo — Logan Paul); cohort scale-out 2024–2026 with the Pump.fun celebrity-launch wave (DADDY / JENNER / MOTHER / DJT)
**Aliases:** "celebrity coin rug", "influencer pump and dump", "celebrity NFT rug", "external-to-crypto promoter dump", "YouTube / X / Twitch celebrity-coin promotion-and-dump"

## Description

Pump-and-dump and rug-pull cases whose **distribution surface is an external-to-crypto influencer's audience reach** — YouTube, X / Twitter, Twitch, podcast, mainstream-celebrity press — rather than a within-crypto coordination channel (Telegram, Discord, DEX-screener leaderboard). Sub-Technique of T3.003 (Coordinated Pump-and-Dump). The structural primitive is *off-platform high-reach social-media-promoter status* — the operator's pre-existing audience is the demand-side amplifier.

T3.004 is **structurally distinct** from sibling T3 sub-Techniques:

- **T3.001 (Sybil Bundled Launch)** — bundler-launch sub-pattern; Sybil-driven concentration at launch. T3.004's distribution-surface dimension is the influencer's audience; the launch may or may not be bundled.
- **T3.002 (Wash Trade Volume Inflation)** — volume-inflation primitive. T3.004 may co-occur with T3.002 but its load-bearing dimension is the influencer-amplification distribution surface.
- **T3.003 (Pump-and-Dump Coordination)** — parent class. T3.003's existing discussion section enumerates Telegram-coordinated channel-administrator sub-pattern (BPS-class), bundler-launch sub-pattern, and DEX-screener-leaderboard sub-pattern; T3.004 adds the influencer-amplified sub-pattern as a fourth structurally-distinct distribution surface.

The structural feature is two-fold:

1. **Distribution-surface amplification by external-to-crypto influencer audience.** The influencer's pre-existing audience (CryptoZoo: Logan Paul's ~24M-follower YouTube audience; KIDS: FaZe Clan's combined audience; DADDY: Andrew Tate's mainstream-controversial reach; JENNER / MOTHER: Caitlyn Jenner / Iggy Azalea celebrity status; DJT: Martin Shkreli's mainstream-political-association reach) is the demand-side amplifier. The promoter does *not* subsequently establish themselves as the launch *operator* — the operator is either a separate party (CryptoZoo's dev team; Sahil Arora for JENNER; Frazier Kay for KIDS) or the operator structure is opaque (DJT).

2. **Bifurcated personal-liability accountability outcome.** Two structurally-distinct accountability outcomes recur across the cohort:
   - The influencer / promoter is typically *insulated* from personal criminal liability through the marketing-fee / contractual-promoter framing or via judicial findings of "puffery" (CryptoZoo October 2025) or "non-security" (JENNER California federal court dismissal).
   - The launch operator (Sahil Arora for JENNER; Frazier Kay for KIDS; Martin Shkreli for DJT; partially-named CryptoZoo dev team) faces civil litigation but rarely produces federal criminal proceedings outside SafeMoon.

The 2021–2024 influencer cohort *predates* the 2024–2026 sovereign / political-endorsement memecoin cohort (HAWK / LIBRA / MELANIA / TRUMP) by 3+ years and is the **structural precedent-substrate** for the political-endorsement cohort.

## Observed indicators

- **Pre-launch promotion** by a non-political celebrity, mainstream-media personality, YouTube creator, or X / Twitter influencer with substantial off-platform reach;
- **Concentrated insider allocation** at launch — pre-promotion buying by a small wallet cluster funded through a single venue (Bubblemaps' canonical DADDY analysis: 21 wallets bought ~30% of DADDY supply *before* Tate's first promotional X post; 11 of those wallets bought ~20% of supply with "nearly identical amounts at the same time" funded through Binance);
- **Promotion-driven demand surge** producing measurable on-chain price / volume / unique-holder-growth alignment with off-platform promotion timing (the promoter's first promotional X post / YouTube video / Twitch stream timestamp typically anchors the demand-surge window);
- **Coordinated insider exit** during or shortly after the demand surge, observable in top-cluster-outflow analysis (Bubblemaps + Lookonchain methodology);
- **Public-record forensic reconstruction** by community investigators (Coffeezilla, ZachXBT, Bubblemaps, Lookonchain) typically lands within days-to-weeks of the launch and forms the structural attribution baseline;
- **Litigation that typically settles or is dismissed without per-celebrity criminal liability** — class-action filings (CryptoZoo February 2023; JENNER federal court action) typically resolve without per-celebrity-promoter criminal proceedings.

## Detection signals

- **On-chain joint-series alignment** of price / volume / unique-holder-growth / top-cluster-outflow during and after the off-platform promotion window. Shared with the existing T3.003 detector but anchored at the influencer-promotion-timestamp window rather than at within-crypto-coordination channel timestamps.
- **Off-platform promoter-graph layer (novel detection axis):** promoter-account activity timestamps; promoter-cohort-cluster membership (the Sahil Arora "middleman" cohort across JENNER + Rich the Kid + Iggy Azalea-impersonation pre-sale); marketing-contract-disclosure regimes; influencer-platform monetisation-program participation.
- **Pre-promotion insider-cluster funding-source analysis:** the canonical Bubblemaps DADDY methodology — pre-promotion wallet-cluster buying with single-source funding — generalises across the cohort.
- **Industry-press cohort tracking:** Coffeezilla long-form investigative coverage (CryptoZoo three-part December 2022 series); Cointelegraph Magazine celebrity-launch-facilitator coverage (Sahil Arora celebrity price-list disclosure); Bubblemaps + Unchained Podcast forensic analysis (MOTHER / Iggy Azalea); ZachXBT on-chain forensic disclosures (DJT / Shkreli deployer attribution).

## Real-world examples

- **Influencer-amplified non-memecoin promotion-and-dump cohort — multi-chain — 2021 onward (canonical T3.004 multi-year cohort)** — Cumulative across the cohort, **$60M+** at low confidence interval, **$200M+** at high confidence interval. Anchor cases:
  - **CryptoZoo (Logan Paul)** — December 2021 launch on Ethereum + Solana; ~$2.5M paid by buyers for "eggs" at minimum per Coffeezilla; class action filed February 2023 with 140 investors claiming losses of $100–$350,000 each (low-CI cohort floor ~$15M; high-CI ceiling ~$50M); lawsuit dismissed October 29, 2025 by Judge Alan D. Albright on "puffery" grounds.
  - **Save the Kids / $KIDS** (FaZe Clan / Frazier "Kay" Khattri / RiceGum / Sommer Ray) — June 2021; peak ~$0.0029 collapsed to ~$0.0012 within a week.
  - **DADDY (Andrew Tate)** — June 2024 launch on Solana via Pump.fun; $45M+ in insider profits sitting on 30% of supply across 21 wallets per Bubblemaps; Tate publicly stated "I will never sell what was sent to my wallet, I will only burn and buy. Forever."
  - **JENNER (Caitlyn Jenner / Sahil Arora)** — May 26, 2024 launch on Solana via Pump.fun; $113.5M trading volume within 4 hours; California federal court dismissed class action on the security-classification ground (does not exonerate launch operators).
  - **MOTHER (Iggy Azalea)** — May 2024; ~$2M+ insider extraction within 24 hours of launch per Bubblemaps + Unchained Podcast investigation; Azalea publicly denied operator role and subsequently launched the Thrust launchpad 2025 explicitly framed as anti-pump-and-dump for celebrity coins.
  - **DJT (Martin Shkreli / claimed Barron Trump association)** — June 2024; ~20% supply held by single wallet dumped causing ~90% price crash; ZachXBT confirmed Shkreli as the deployer via on-chain forensics; Arkham bounty paid for the deployer attribution.

  Attribution: `mixed` (per-case strength labels follow). **Confirmed**-via-legal-record for: SafeMoon (federal SEC + DOJ charges November 2023 — cited at T3.003 already); CryptoZoo (class action filed February 2023, dismissed October 2025 with judicial finding of "puffery" rather than fraud — the dismissal is itself a public-record outcome and is informative about the cohort's accountability landscape). **Inferred-strong** for: DADDY (Bubblemaps wallet-cluster analysis + on-chain insider-extraction signal); JENNER (Sahil Arora self-attribution + multiple-celebrity public allegations + Cointelegraph Magazine documentation); MOTHER (Bubblemaps + Unchained Podcast investigation). **Inferred-strong-with-pseudonymity-caveat** for DJT (ZachXBT on-chain forensics confirmed Shkreli as deployer; claimed Barron Trump collaboration without evidence; Roger Stone publicly denied Trump-family involvement). **Inferred-medium** for Save the Kids (Frazier Kay's wallet activity reconstructed by Coffeezilla; FaZe Clan publicly expelled Kay and suspended three members; no federal charges public). ## Mitigations

- **Off-platform promoter-graph monitoring (highest-leverage novel mitigation):** track promoter-account activity timestamps for influencers known to engage in token / NFT promotion; track promoter-cohort-cluster membership (the Sahil Arora "middleman" cohort across JENNER + Rich the Kid + Iggy Azalea-impersonation pre-sale is the canonical cohort fingerprint); track marketing-contract-disclosure regimes where applicable (FTC endorsement disclosure compliance; SEC promoter-rule application).
- **Pre-promotion insider-cluster detection:** Bubblemaps-style wallet-cluster funding-source analysis surfaced *before* the influencer's first promotional post can be a leading indicator of operator-side insider extraction. The canonical DADDY case had 11 wallets buy ~20% of supply with "nearly identical amounts at the same time" via Binance funding *before* Tate's promotion — a public-disclosure-time detection opportunity.
- **Influencer-platform monetisation-program participation auditability:** YouTube / X / Twitch / podcast monetisation programs that surface influencer promotional history in a publicly-auditable form discipline the cohort by raising the cost-of-future-promotion for known offenders. The pattern recurs across SafeMoon (federal action) and the CryptoZoo / KIDS / DADDY / JENNER cohort (civil-litigation disclosure).
- **Industry-press cohort framing:** long-form investigative coverage (Coffeezilla; Unchained; Cointelegraph Magazine; ZachXBT public disclosures) is the load-bearing accountability primitive for the cohort given the structural insulation of celebrity-promoters from federal criminal liability. Cohort-level user-awareness is the secondary effect.
- **Anti-pump-and-dump launchpad infrastructure:** the Iggy Azalea / Thrust launchpad case (2025) is the canonical example of an in-cohort response — a celebrity who experienced reputational damage from a launch later launching anti-pump-and-dump infrastructure for celebrity coins. Pattern is operationally informative but not yet a load-bearing cohort-level mitigation primitive.

## Discussion

T3.004 closes a structural gap in OAK v0.1's T3 coverage. The T3.003 parent class enumerates Telegram-coordinated, bundler-launch, and DEX-screener-leaderboard sub-patterns in its discussion section; T3.004 adds the influencer-amplified sub-pattern as a fourth structurally-distinct distribution surface.

The structural OAK lesson is that **external-to-crypto influencer audience reach is a distinct distribution-surface class** for pump-and-dump operations, qualitatively different from within-crypto coordination channels (Telegram, Discord, DEX-screener leaderboard). The detection signal at the on-chain layer is shared with T3.003 (joint-series alignment of price / volume / unique-holder-growth / top-cluster-outflow); the *novel detection axis* is the off-platform promoter-graph layer.

The bifurcated personal-liability accountability outcome is structurally important for OAK's framework-credibility-vs-realised-accountability framing. The cohort's defining accountability outcome is the *insulation of the celebrity-promoter from personal criminal liability* through marketing-fee / contractual-promoter framing or via judicial findings of "puffery" (CryptoZoo) or "non-security" (JENNER). The structural exception is SafeMoon (federal SEC + DOJ charges November 2023, see T3.003) and the partial parallel of LIBRA where Argentine prosecutors opened criminal proceedings.

The 2021–2024 influencer cohort is the **structural precedent-substrate** for the 2024–2026 sovereign / political-endorsement memecoin cohort (HAWK / LIBRA / MELANIA / TRUMP). The political-endorsement cohort's structural pattern — endorser-operator-bifurcation, insider-cluster pre-promotion buying, demand surge from off-platform amplification, coordinated insider exit, civil-litigation-without-criminal-proceedings — was established in the influencer cohort 3+ years earlier. T3.004 contributors should preserve the structural-precedent-substrate dimension explicitly and frame the political-endorsement cohort as a downstream variant rather than a structurally-novel class.

The Sahil Arora "middleman" / celebrity-token-launch-facilitator cohort is a candidate operator-cluster for future OAK-G entry promotion at v0.x — Arora's Wikipedia entry documents his role across JENNER, Rich the Kid, and adjacent celebrity launches; Cointelegraph Magazine published a leaked celebrity price-list for Arora's services. The Hayden Davis / Kelsier Ventures cluster is a separate documented cohort overlapping the celebrity-endorsement memecoin space (see [`examples/2025-01-hayden-davis-kelsier-cluster.md`](../examples/2025-01-hayden-davis-kelsier-cluster.md)); the cases here are non-Kelsier and pre-date the Kelsier cohort's publicly-named launches.

The DJT case is structurally distinctive within the cohort. ZachXBT confirmed Martin Shkreli as the deployer via on-chain forensics; Shkreli publicly claimed collaboration with Barron Trump but provided no evidence; Roger Stone publicly denied Trump-family involvement. The case occupies the boundary between the influencer-amplified sub-pattern (T3.004) and the political-endorsement sub-pattern (HAWK / LIBRA / MELANIA / TRUMP cohort) — Shkreli's mainstream-political-association reach is the demand-side amplifier even though the political-endorsement claim is unsubstantiated. Future T3.004 worked examples should preserve the boundary-case dimension and avoid forcing the case into either sub-pattern bucket cleanly.

Promotion to `stable` will follow once the per-incident worked-example anchor set expands beyond the current single multi-year cohort file (CryptoZoo-as-standalone, KIDS-as-standalone, DADDY-as-standalone, JENNER-as-standalone, MOTHER-as-standalone, DJT-as-standalone). The current 2021–2026 cohort file is sufficient to anchor T3.004 at `emerging` per the v0.1 promotion criteria but is structurally a single composite anchor rather than the multi-anchor independent-confirmation pattern that warrants `stable`.
