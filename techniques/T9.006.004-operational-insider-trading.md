# OAK-T9.006.004 — Operational-Insider Trading on Subjective-Resolution Prediction Markets

**Parent Tactics:** OAK-T9 (Smart-Contract Exploit), OAK-T17 (Market Manipulation); sub-Technique of OAK-T9.006 (Subjective-Oracle Resolution Manipulation)
**Maturity:** emerging
**Chains:** EVM (Polymarket on Polygon canonical at v0.1)
**First documented:** 2025-12 (Van Dyke / Operation Absolute Resolve betting window) → 2026-01 (real-world execution) → 2026-04 (DOJ SDNY indictment + CFTC parallel civil action). Multi-jurisdictional confirmation 2026-02 (Tel Aviv District Court indictment of IDF reservist).
**Aliases:** "operational-insider Polymarket", "classified-information prediction-market trading", "causal-actor insider trading"

## Description

Attack on the **information-symmetry assumption between bettors** in subjective-resolution prediction markets. A participant with classified-information access to (and operational involvement in) the underlying real-world event trades on that knowledge before public disclosure. The bettor is not just *informed* about the outcome — they are one of the **causal actors producing it**.

This crosses from classical insider trading (informational asymmetry) into a class structurally adjacent to market-manipulation-via-action: the bettor's operational decisions on the world (when the raid is launched, on what date, with what force composition) directly determine which resolution outcome triggers their payout.

Distinct from sibling sub-Techniques:

- **T9.006.001 (DVM Vote Capture)** — attacks the oracle layer; resolution input is honest, the vote is corrupted. T9.006.004 leaves the oracle layer untouched; the resolution is correct given the public information available at the resolution time, but the attacker traded ahead of that information.
- **T9.006.002 (Resolution-Spec Ambiguity Exploitation)** — attacks the spec layer. T9.006.004 leaves the spec untouched.
- **T9.006.003 (Off-chain Resolution-Source Coercion)** — pressures third-party reporters post hoc. T9.006.004 acts on the underlying real-world event itself, before disclosure.

The on-chain settlement substrate makes T9.006.004 **qualitatively different from traditional Section 10(b)/Rule 10b-5 insider trading**:

1. **Position and timing are publicly inspectable.** Wallet address, deposit timestamp, share-acquisition ladder, and withdrawal pattern are all on-chain and forensically reconstructable by prosecutors. Traditional insider-trading detection relies on broker subpoenas; on-chain prediction markets give the regulator the trade record by default.
2. **Wallet-attribution is the load-bearing forensic step.** Real-world identification depends on KYC at the off-ramp (Kraken / brokerage) plus account-creation metadata. The on-chain data alone does not name the operator; it names the position.
3. **Geofencing is partial.** Polymarket nominally bars US persons; the participation pattern (US-based active-duty soldier creating an account) demonstrates that VPN-and-self-attestation geofencing does not survive a determined participant who has every motivation to conceal jurisdiction.
4. **The market itself surfaces the anomaly.** Sharp longshot wins by newly-created accounts in narrow time windows are detectable signatures; the public record now contains explicit baseline rates (ACDC analytics: longshots win ~14% baseline, ~51.8% in military / defense markets) that establish what counts as anomaly.

## Observed indicators

- New Polymarket account creation in narrow time window before a classified real-world event.
- Concentration of bets on YES-side of correlated markets gated by the classified event.
- Sharp longshot wins by newly-created accounts in narrow time windows.
- Same-day withdrawal of profits before public Polymarket-side scrutiny ramps up.
- Operational-actor involvement in the underlying real-world event (Special Forces NCO; reservist with classified briefing access; intelligence-cleared personnel).
- KYC linkage at off-ramp (Kraken / brokerage account) plus account-creation metadata correlating to cleared-personnel identity.

## Detection signals

- **Account-age-vs-first-bet correlation** on markets gated by classified events.
- **Sharp longshot wins concentrated in newly-created accounts** on correlated cohorts of markets.
- **KYC linkage at the off-ramp** (Kraken / brokerage) plus account-creation metadata.
- **Counter-intelligence cross-reference** on operationally-involved actors when sharp longshot wins are detected on correlated military / defense markets.
- **ACDC analytics baseline rates** as published reference for cohort-anomaly detection (longshots: ~14% baseline, ~51.8% in military / defense markets).
- **Polymarket / Chainalysis surveillance partnership** (operationalised April 2026) as the cohort-tracking surface.

## Real-world examples

- **Polymarket / Van Dyke / Venezuela / Operation Absolute Resolve — 2025-12 → 2026-01 → 2026-04 (canonical T9.006.004 case)** — ~\$33,000 wagered → ~\$404,000–\$410,000 in profits extracted. US Army Special Forces master sergeant Gannon Ken Van Dyke, operationally involved in the planning and execution of Operation Absolute Resolve (Maduro capture, January 3, 2026), opened a Polymarket account December 26, 2025 and placed ~13 bets totalling ~\$33,034 on YES sides of Venezuela / Maduro markets December 27 – January 2. Bets resolved YES January 3–4; on the day of the operation, Van Dyke withdrew the majority of proceeds, routed funds through a foreign cryptocurrency vault, and deposited them into a newly-created online brokerage account. Arrested April 2026; DOJ SDNY indictment for unlawful use of confidential government information for personal gain, theft of nonpublic government information, commodities fraud, wire fraud, and engaging in a monetary transaction in property derived from specified unlawful activity (up to 20 years on the wire-fraud count). Pleaded not guilty April 28, 2026. CFTC filed parallel civil enforcement action. Kalshi publicly stated it had blocked the same individual on its venue. **First publicly documented case in crypto-prediction-market history** in which a bettor with direct operational involvement in the underlying real-world event has been criminally charged. See [`examples/2026-01-polymarket-venezuela-maduro-soldier-insider.md`](../examples/2026-01-polymarket-venezuela-maduro-soldier-insider.md).
- **Polymarket / IDF reservist / Iran-strike timing — 2025-06 → 2026-02 (T9.006.004 cohort case, multi-jurisdictional)** — ~\$162,663 in profits extracted by a two-person scheme. Israeli Air Force major (reservist) briefed civilian co-defendant when warplanes were already en route to Iranian targets; civilian placed Polymarket bets on the timing of Israel's June 2025 opening strike. Pair extracted ~\$162,663, agreed to split evenly. Tel Aviv District Court indictment February 2026; charges include severe security offences, bribery, obstruction of justice. The pair continued betting across additional Iran-conflict markets (including a winning bet on the end-date of the 12-day war) until arrest. **Establishes T9.006.004 as multi-jurisdictional (US + IL) within a four-month window.** The Israeli case differs structurally from Van Dyke in one important way: the briefing was passed laterally to a civilian co-conspirator who placed the actual bets, decoupling the on-chain wallet identity from the cleared-personnel identity. Purely on-chain detection (account-age, deposit-time, bet-cluster) does not by itself identify the operational insider; the linkage runs through human intelligence on the briefing chain. See [`examples/2026-02-polymarket-iran-strike-idf-reservist-insider.md`](../examples/2026-02-polymarket-iran-strike-idf-reservist-insider.md).
- **Polymarket US election operational-insider trading cohort — 2024–2025 (T9.006.004 domain-expansion anchor).** A cohort of Polymarket accounts with apparent access to non-public campaign-internal data, polling, or operational-timeline knowledge placed bets on US election-related markets with anomalous longshot win-rates (~51.8% in political/defense markets vs. ~14% baseline). The cohort establishes that T9.006.004 generalises beyond military-operational contexts (Van Dyke, IDF reservist) to any domain where non-public information about a real-world event determines a prediction-market resolution — including political campaigns, corporate announcements, and regulatory decisions. The on-chain forensic surface (wallet address, deposit timestamp, position ladder, withdrawal pattern) is domain-agnostic. See [`examples/2025-01-polymarket-us-election-insider-trading-cohort.md`](../examples/2025-01-polymarket-us-election-insider-trading-cohort.md).

## Mitigations

- **Account-age-vs-first-bet correlation cohort surveillance** on markets gated by classified events.
- **Sharp-longshot-win cohort surveillance** with ACDC-class baseline rates as the reference threshold.
- **KYC linkage at off-ramp** as the load-bearing forensic step for wallet-to-real-world identification.
- **Cross-venue blocklist coordination** (Kalshi / Polymarket / future prediction-market venues) for identified operational-insider actors.
- **Counter-intelligence integration** for operationally-involved-actor cross-reference on flagged cohorts.
- **Geofencing reinforcement:** Polymarket's nominal bar on US persons via VPN-and-self-attestation is structurally insufficient; the operational-insider cohort has every incentive to evade. Stronger geofencing at the transaction-acceptance layer (rather than at the registration-self-attestation layer) is the structural mitigation candidate, but operationally hard for a fully-on-chain venue.

## Discussion

T9.006.004 is structurally the most novel of the four T9.006 sub-patterns at v0.1 because the bettor is not just *informed* about the outcome — they are one of the **causal actors producing it**. This crosses from classical insider trading into a class structurally adjacent to market-manipulation-via-action.

**Note on T9.006.004 ID framing.** An earlier `TAXONOMY-GAPS.md` framing of T9.006.004 used a different sub-pattern (platform-override of oracle outcome — Barron Trump / DJT memecoin market). At v0.1 promotion the existing example anchors (Van Dyke / Venezuela; IDF reservist / Iran-strike) self-categorise as T9.006.004 with the operational-insider definition; the platform-override sub-pattern lacks a worked-example anchor at v0.1 and is held as a v0.x candidate for a separate sub-Technique slot. T9.006.004 is canonical for the operational-insider sub-pattern at v0.1 freeze; contributors documenting future platform-override cases should propose a separate sub-Technique ID (e.g. T9.006.005) at v0.x.

**On-chain settlement substrate creates novel forensic affordances.** The on-chain transparency that traditional finance lacks (wallet, timestamp, position ladder, withdrawal pattern publicly inspectable) makes T9.006.004 cases forensically tractable in ways that traditional Section 10(b)/Rule 10b-5 cases are not. The Van Dyke case was driven by a combination of public on-chain forensics (Bubblemaps, Sonar Pro, NPR-cited analytics firms initially flagging the ~\$400K profit cluster as anomalous) plus federal subpoenas to Polymarket and Kraken for KYC linkage, plus Defense-side records matching Van Dyke's role to bet timing.

**Multi-jurisdictional pattern is established.** The Van Dyke (US, April 2026) and IDF reservist (Israel, February 2026) cases together establish T9.006.004 as a multi-jurisdictional structural class within a four-month window. The pattern is now multi-jurisdictionally documented; future cases are likely to continue surfacing as KYC-cluster forensics become standard at prediction-market venues and at off-ramp exchanges.

**The IDF reservist case demonstrates a forensically harder sub-shape:** the briefing was passed laterally to a civilian co-conspirator who placed the actual bets. This decouples the on-chain wallet identity from the cleared-personnel identity. Purely on-chain detection does not identify the operational insider; the linkage runs through human intelligence on the briefing chain. Contributors writing future T9.006.004 worked examples should expect this lateral-disclosure sub-shape to recur as operational-insider trading scales, and should record the briefing-chain dimension as a first-class observable.

**The mitigation surface is partly off-chain.** Account-age-vs-first-bet correlation, KYC at off-ramp, cross-venue blocklist coordination, and counter-intelligence cross-reference are all real defender mitigations, but the load-bearing prevention is on the operational-actor side (security clearance briefings on prediction-market trading risk; clearance-loss as deterrent; pre-trade compliance enforcement at military / intelligence personnel offboarding events). Polymarket / Chainalysis surveillance partnership (operationalised April 2026) is the post-event detection layer; the prevention layer requires off-platform coordination.

Promotion to `stable` will follow once T9.006.004 carries additional cohort cases beyond the Van Dyke / IDF reservist anchor. The pipeline includes any 2026+ DOJ / CFTC / international prosecution of operational-insider trading on subjective-resolution prediction markets.
