# OAK-T3.003 — Coordinated Pump-and-Dump

**Parent Tactics:** OAK-T3 (Holder Capture), OAK-T5 (Value Extraction)
**Maturity:** stable
**Chains:** EVM, Solana, BSC, CEX (cross-venue)
**First documented:** 2017–2018 (Telegram-group studies); 2021 (Bitwise CEX-cohort context); systematic 2024–2025
**Aliases:** "P&D", "coordinated pump", "group pump", "shill-coordinated dump"

## Description

A pattern in which a coordinated set of participants amplify a token's apparent demand within a short window, then exit positions while later entrants are still acquiring. From a defender's perspective, the Technique is identifiable through the **temporal joint distribution** of price, volume, holder growth, and concentrated-cluster outflow — none alone is conclusive; their joint pattern is.

OAK v0.1 distinguishes four operational sub-patterns under T3.003. They share the price/volume/cluster-outflow joint signature but differ in the coordination surface and the operator-discovery approach. A v0.x sub-Technique split is plausible once each sub-pattern has independent calibration evidence:

- **Telegram / Discord / X coordinated pumps** — group-channel-organised buy-and-dump windows; canonical academic cohort per `[bolz2024]` (~2,079 historical pumps via NLP over Telegram messages).
- **Pump.fun bundler-launch dumps** — cluster acquires majority supply at launch (T3.001), promotes via social channels, dumps into retail entry; the bundler-launch and the dump are often within hours.
- **Celebrity-shill pumps** — promoter cohort with off-platform reach (YouTube / X / Twitch) acquires pre-launch allocations, promotes a launch, dumps into the resulting volume.
- **DEX-screener / leaderboard manipulation** — coordinated buy-window optimised to push a token onto trending / top-mover surfaces (DEX Screener, GeckoTerminal, CoinMarketCap); dump follows once organic traffic arrives.

## Observed indicators

- Sharp upward price move on rising volume, followed by net outflow from a small early-holder cluster while retail entry continues.
- Unique-holder count plateaus or declines while volume spikes.
- External signal correlation: promotional activity in venues known for coordinated promotion preceding the price move (Telegram channels, X accounts with prior P&D history, celebrity-shill posts).
- Cluster-outflow timing: top-holder cluster reduces position by a characteristic fraction (commonly 30–60%) within minutes-to-hours of peak price.
- Cross-token recurrence: same cluster previously associated with similar P&D patterns on other tokens (T8.001 link).
- For DEX-screener manipulation: volume bursts narrowly aligned to ranking-snapshot windows.

## Detection signals

- **Joint-series alignment:** align price / volume / unique-holder-growth / top-cluster-outflow on the same time axis; flag windows where top-cluster outflow leads price decline by characteristic lag (commonly 5–60 minutes for memecoin pumps).
- **Cluster-outflow / price-decline correlation query (Dune-style):** `with cluster_out as (select block_time, sum(amount) from transfers where from_address in (select addr from cluster_X)) select corr(cluster_out.amount, price_decline) over (order by block_time rows between 60 preceding and current row)`
- **External-signal correlation:** ingest Telegram-channel and X-account activity timestamps; flag price-spike windows preceded by coordinated promotional posts within calibrated lag (per `[bolz2024]` real-time NLP detector).
- **Cross-token recurrence (T8.001 link):** maintain per-cluster history of prior P&D-flagged events; alert when a recurring cluster appears in a new token's top-holder set.
- **Forta / Solana-program-monitor triggers:** subscribe to top-cluster-position-decline events; on threshold breach with rising volume, post to alert sink.

## Real-world examples

- **SafeMoon — BSC — 2021 onward; SEC/DOJ charges November 2023.** Per `[secsafemoon2023]`, SafeMoon executives are alleged to have misappropriated >$200M from the project; the SEC complaint specifically alleges that CEO John Karony used a trading account to buy and sell SafeMoon to create the impression of market activity (wash sub-pattern) alongside the broader pump-and-dump structure (promotion of "safely to the moon" while operators withdrew liquidity and proceeds). The case is the highest-profile U.S.-government-pursued T3.003 prosecution at v0.1 and anchors the celebrity-and-influencer-promoted variant of the Technique.
- **Save the Kids ($KIDS) — BSC / PancakeSwap — June 2021.** FaZe Clan members (Kay, Jarvis, Nikan, Teeqo) and other influencers (RiceGum, Sommer Ray) promoted a launch on June 5, 2021; the token collapsed within hours as promoters dumped pre-launch allocations. Per Coffeezilla's investigation, Frazier Kay sold nearly his entire allocation; price fell from ~$0.02 to ~$0.00138 within weeks. FaZe Clan expelled Kay and suspended three other members. ## Mitigations

- **Traders / risk teams:** treat sharp moves on small-cap tokens with concentrated holders as elevated risk; require holder-growth confirmation before entry. See OAK-M04 (Funder-Graph Clustering).
- **Venues:** rate-limit aggressive promotional listings; publish concentration metrics alongside price feeds. See OAK-M26 (Wash-Trade Rate Metrics) for the related metric-display layer.
- **Aggregators / leaderboards (DEX Screener, GeckoTerminal, CoinMarketCap):** apply cluster-aware volume adjustments to ranking inputs to neutralise the leaderboard-manipulation sub-pattern at the source.
- **Regulators / enforcement:** the SafeMoon prosecution demonstrates that named-defendant T3.003 cases are pursuable when misappropriation and wash-trading are jointly evidenced; the per-incident attribution surface improves with operator-cluster forensic data.

## Discussion

Distinguishing "coordinated promotion" from "organic momentum" is fundamentally probabilistic; v0.2 will introduce a confidence-band reporting convention for P&D detectors. False-positive sources: a popular project's organic launch can produce price/volume signatures superficially similar to coordinated P&D; the discriminator is the cluster-outflow pattern (organic launches do not typically produce concentrated cluster outflow at peak) and external-signal corroboration.

The four sub-pattern split (Telegram-coordinated / pump.fun bundler-launch / celebrity-shill / leaderboard-manipulation) is a v0.x candidate for separate sub-Techniques. At v0.1 the unified framing is justified because the joint-series detection signature is shared. Sub-pattern-specific detectors (Telegram-NLP per `[bolz2024]`; bundler-funder-graph per `[liu2025sybil]`; off-chain promoter-graph for celebrity-shill) operate at different ingest layers but consume the same on-chain joint series for confirmation.

Calibration gap: the celebrity-shill sub-pattern has weaker public detector coverage than the Telegram or bundler sub-patterns because the off-platform promotion graph is harder to ingest at scale; the SafeMoon and Save-the-Kids cases were uncovered primarily through investigative-journalism (Coffeezilla) rather than automated detection. v0.x should publish a promoter-graph ingest convention.
